Got Emergency? We got you.

Though we do not allow unrestricted posting on our platform, we understand the importance of community participation. Therefore, we have implemented an internal posting option for all users. This allows individuals to submit content to be reviewed and approved by our team. The review process usually takes a few hours and once approved, the content will be publicly visible on the platform. Additionally, we actively monitor contributions from users and may automatically verify their account for full posting privileges. This is a measure to ensure the authenticity and credibility of the content on our platform and to maintain a safe and trustworthy community for our users.

Building a better world

At PetUs

We believe in coexistence and the adoption of pets. We firmly believe that adopting a pet from a shelter is a more responsible and ethical choice than buying one from a breeder or pet store. Not only does adopting a pet save a life, but it also helps to reduce overpopulation in shelters and the euthanasia of healthy animals. By choosing to adopt a pet, we can make a positive impact on the lives of animals and our community. Furthermore, adopting a pet can also bring much joy and love into our own lives. We encourage everyone to consider adopting a pet instead of buying one and to support their local animal shelters in any way they can. At PetUs, we are committed to promoting the adoption of pets and we will continue to support animal shelters and rescue organizations in their efforts to find loving homes for animals in need.

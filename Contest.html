Humanity first contest : August

Being a good citizen should amount to something and with this thought today we announce Humanity First our monthly contest at PetUs for the month August-September.
All you have to do is download PetUs app and click the maximum pictures of dogs and upload on PetUs


What will the winner get ?
1. A $10 prize just, for clicking normal pictures of dogs from your phone and uploading them on PetUs app.
2. verified account on PetUs under dog census
3. If you are a working in animal welfare on daily basis you will automatically be eligilbe for emergency funds for the future.

 

Rules:

 

1. Upload the dogs near you at PetUs

2. At least 50 photo uploads are needed to qualify.

        2.1 Only 1 photo per dog will be counted.

3. You should be following PetUs's Instagram and twitter.

4.  The participation is free for all. 

The contest will run till September 10 and the results will be announced on September 15.
 

Note: Please use your official email ID as it will be the mode of communication if you win the contest.
 

And you are all set to go!!
